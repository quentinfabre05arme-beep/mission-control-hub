## 🔄 DAILY SKILL PRACTICE

### Morning (Pre-Market)
1. **Review overnight developments** (15 min)
   - News scan
   - Price action review
   - Alert check

2. **Study session** (30 min)
   - Book chapter or framework
   - Pattern recognition drill
   - Case study review

3. **Journal preparation** (15 min)
   - Set learning goals for day
   - Prepare decision templates
   - Review open positions

### During Market Hours
4. **Active observation** (ongoing)
   - Note price patterns
   - Track narrative shifts
   - Journal emotional states

5. **Decision execution** (as needed)
   - Use checklist for entries
   - Document thesis
   - Commit to stops

### Evening (Post-Market)
6. **Review and extraction** (30 min)
   - Update pattern library
   - Journal today's decisions
   - Note questions for research

7. **Study and preparation** (30 min)
   - Read earnings transcripts
   - Research questions from day
   - Plan next day's focus

**Total Daily Learning Time: ~2 hours**
