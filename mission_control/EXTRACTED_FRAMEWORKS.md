**I. Business Quality (25 points)**
- Moat strength (0-10)

- Financial health (0-5)

**II. Valuation Attractiveness (25 points)**

**III. Catalyst Visibility (25 points)**
- Near-term catalysts (0-10)

**IV. Risk Assessment (25 points)**
- Downside scenarios (0-10)

## 🎯 FRAMEWORK 5: WHEN TO SELL

### From: Vol 6 (Practitioner) - Feroldi Framework

#### Three Reasons to Sell

**1. Thesis Violation** (Fundamental)
- Original investment case no longer valid
- Competitive advantage eroding
- Management actions contradict thesis

**Your Application:**
- Document thesis before entry
- Quarterly thesis review
- Sell if thesis breaks (regardless of price)

**2. Valuation Excess** (Price)
- Price exceeds intrinsic value by margin of safety
- Better opportunities available
- Rebalancing needed

**Your Application:**
- Scale out at +10%, +20%, +30%
- Full exit if >40% over fair value
- Rotate to higher-conviction idea

**3. Portfolio Management** (Risk)
- Position becomes too large (>15%)
- Correlation increases unexpectedly
- Liquidity needs

**Your Application:**
- Trim at 15% of portfolio
- Reduce if correlation >0.8 with existing positions
- Maintain 44% max deployed

#### The "Never Sell" Exception
**For Core Holdings (BTC/ETH):**
- Never sell based on price alone
- Only sell if thesis fundamentally changes
- Use as collateral instead of selling
