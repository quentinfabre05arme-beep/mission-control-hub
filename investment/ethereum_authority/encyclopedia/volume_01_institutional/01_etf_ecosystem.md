# Ethereum ETF Ecosystem

## Overview
Ethereum ETFs represent the primary institutional on-ramp for ETH exposure. Understanding flow dynamics, creation/redemption mechanics, and market impact is critical for timing and positioning.

### Flow Dynamics
- **Creation**: Authorized Participants (APs) deposit ETH → receive ETF shares
- **Redemption**: APs return shares → receive ETH
- **Premium/Discount**: ETF price vs NAV arbitrage opportunity

## Historical Context

### Launch Period (Jul-Aug 2024)
- Initial inflows: ~$2B in first 60 days
- ETHE bleed: Grayscale conversion outflows ~$3B
- Net effect: Positive but choppy

### Key Metrics to Track
1. **Daily flows** — Farside Investors tracker
2. **Creation units** — Large creations signal institutional demand
3. **Premium/discount** — Real-time sentiment gauge
4. **Correlation to spot** — How well ETFs track ETH

## Investment Implications

### Bullish Scenarios
- Sustained daily inflows >$100M
- ETHE outflows slowing/stopping
- Premium developing (demand > supply)
- New issuer launches (marketing spend)

### Bearish Scenarios  
- Outflows persist >30 days
- ETHE discount widening
- Correlation breakdown during stress
- Regulatory uncertainty
