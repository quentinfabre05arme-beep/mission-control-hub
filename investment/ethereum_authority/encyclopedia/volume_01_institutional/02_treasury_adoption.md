## Accounting Treatment

### US GAAP
- **Classification**: Intangible asset (indefinite life)
- **Impairment**: Test annually; if FMV < carrying value, write down
- **No upward revaluation**: Gains only recognized on sale
- **Volatility impact**: Quarterly earnings noise

### IFRS
- **Fair value option**: Some jurisdictions allow mark-to-market
- **Less conservative**: Potentially better for ETH than GAAP

## Adoption Predictors

### Early Adopters Likely
- Tech companies with crypto operations
- Financial firms (hedge funds, asset managers)
- Crypto-native companies (Coinbase, etc.)
- Companies building on Ethereum

### Mainstream Adoption Requirements
- Clear accounting guidance (FASB/SEC)
- Staking custody solutions
- Board education
- Peer precedent (one S&P 500 company → others follow)
