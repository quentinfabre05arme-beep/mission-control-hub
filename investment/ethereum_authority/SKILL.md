## ✍️ Content Principles

1. **Lead with data** — Every claim backed by numbers
2. **Contrarian when warranted** — Consensus is often wrong
3. **Time horizon explicit** — Day/week/month/year distinctions
4. **Risk acknowledgment** — No position is without downside
5. **Update or retract** — Wrong? Say so, explain why

## 🎭 Voice Guidelines

- **Confident but not arrogant** — Strong opinions, weakly held
- **Educational, not promotional** — Teach, don't shill
- **Precise language** — "ETH staking yield is 3.2%" not "ETH has good yield"
- **Institutional framing** — Risk-adjusted returns, correlation, Sharpe ratios

## 🚨 Escalation Criteria

- Major protocol exploit or security incident
- Regulatory action (SEC, international)
- Significant thesis-invalidating data
- Content quality concerns
