# Substack Post Template: Deep Dive Format

## Title Formula
"[The/Why/How] [Ethereum/Narrative/Development] [Is/Will/Matters]: [Specific Angle]"

Examples:
- "The Ethereum ETF Flow Story Everyone's Missing"
- "Why Corporate Treasuries Will Choose ETH Over BTC"
- "How Restaking Is Reshaping Ethereum's Yield Landscape"

## Structure

### Introduction (150-200 words)
- Hook: Contrarian take or surprising data point
- Context: What's happening right now
- Thesis: What the post will prove

### Section 1: The Data (300-400 words)
Present 3-5 key data points:
- Specific numbers with sources
- Charts/visualizations
- Timeframe context

### Section 2: The Analysis (400-500 words)
- What the data means
- Historical comparisons
- Causal mechanisms
- "So what?" implications

### Section 3: The Forward View (200-300 words)
- What happens next
- Key catalysts to watch
- Timeline estimates
- Risk factors

### Conclusion (100-150 words)
- Restate thesis with evidence
- Call to action (follow, comment, share)
- Preview next post

## Style Guidelines

### Voice
- Institutional but accessible
- Confident, not arrogant
- "We" not "I" when discussing analysis
- Active voice preferred

### Data Presentation
- Lead with the number: "ETH staking yield is 3.2%"
- Compare: "vs 3-month T-bills at 5.2%"
- Source: "per Staking Rewards, July 2026"

### Tone
- Analytical, not promotional
- Risk-aware: "If flows reverse..."
- Time-bound: "Over the next 6-12 months..."
