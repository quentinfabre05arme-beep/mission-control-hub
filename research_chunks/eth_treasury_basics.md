# ETH Treasury Basics

## Key Stats
- 19 public companies hold ETH treasuries
- ~3M ETH held ($13.2B value)
- Staking yield: 3-4% APR
- BTC treasuries: 0% yield

## Why ETH Over BTC for Treasuries

**BTC = Store of Value**
- Digital gold narrative
- No yield generation
- Must sell to realize gains
- Strategy sold $216M BTC (first ever sale)

**ETH = Productive Capital**
- Staking generates 3-4% annually
- Network fees create deflationary pressure
- Settlement layer for DeFi/finance
- Can fund operations without selling

## Institutional Adoption
- Franklin Templeton: ETH ETFs approved
- BlackRock: Tokenized funds on ETH
- Major banks: Repo/trading on blockchain

## The Math
$10M ETH staking = $300K-$400K annual income
$10M BTC = $0 income

## Thesis
Next-gen treasuries hold productive assets, not just store of value.
