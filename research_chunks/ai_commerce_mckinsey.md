# AI Agentic Commerce

## The McKinsey Projection
$3-5 trillion in agentic commerce by 2030

## What Is Agentic Commerce?
AI agents autonomously conducting commerce:
- Negotiating deals
- Managing supply chains
- Personal shopping
- Dynamic pricing
- Inventory optimization

## Why It Matters
- US B2C alone: $1T+ opportunity
- Not just chatbots — autonomous economic actors
- Changes how business happens

## The Winners
Not LLM makers (OpenAI, Anthropic)
Intelligence companies that own customer relationships:
- Data moats
- Distribution
- Trust/brand

## Timeline
- 2024-2025: Early adoption
- 2026-2028: Mainstream integration
- 2030: $3-5T fully deployed

## Portfolio Implications
- NBIS, PLTR, ZETA, etc.
- Infrastructure > applications
- Early = asymmetric returns

## Thesis
Biggest investment opportunity of the decade.
