# HIMS Business Model

## What HIMS Actually Is
Not a drug company. Healthcare infrastructure for the GLP-1 revolution.

## Key Metrics
- Revenue: $2.35B (+59% YoY)
- Novo Nordisk partnership: Locked
- Business model: Telehealth rails for drug delivery

## The GLP-1 Revolution
- Weight loss drugs (Ozempic, Wegovy) changing healthcare
- Market focuses on the drugs
- Real play: Infrastructure to deliver them

## Why HIMS Wins
- Picks and shovels vs. betting on drug winners
- Platform for ongoing care, not one-time prescriptions
- Telehealth = scalable, low overhead
- Regulatory moat in controlled substance delivery

## Competitive Position
- CVS/traditional healthcare: Legacy, slow
- HIMS: Digital-native, fast, consumer-focused
- GLP-1 delivery requires ongoing management → recurring revenue

## Thesis
Infrastructure beats products in platform shifts.
